About Me

I'm Oli Shepherd, a start-up co-founder and founder who has brought two systems to market and I'm working on my third. I've been a COO at SaaS companies for a number of years, selling B2G, B2B and B2C and now I'm playing at building software myself to build my skills. My first career was as a surveyor and asset manager.

I've put my portfolio on here and the links will mainly lead you to my passion project: ProperGander. I'm working on breaking down the media's narratives. I'll be doing critical analysis of the news whilst we build the ProperGander app to get to the truth of the story using AI and Machine Learning.

I live with my beautiful wife and two crazy little boys in South West Surrey. I'm constantly learning about business, society and, recently, the guitar (you don't want to hear it). To get the blood going I love mountain biking, tennis and skiing.
